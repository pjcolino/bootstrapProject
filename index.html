About Our Club


Our club specialises in the listening of jazz, both old standards and modern vibes. We meet once a week to listern, learn, and discuss, all surrounded by fellow jazz connoisseurs.


Our Values


MISSION: Our mission is to provide a space for jazz enthusiasts to enjoy music both old and new. We meet twice a month in the Seattle Academy bandroom to listen to old recordings, and we bring it a live concert once a month with artists from, or visiting the Seattle Area. Snacks, drinks and food always offered.
